Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kyoto Tourist Map. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Location Data

This application requests access to your device's location (GPS) to show your current position on the map and to find nearby facilities. This location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers. We do not track your movement history.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap & Overpass API: To provide map data and location information.
  • Google Maps: For navigation (external link).
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements.

4. Cookies and Ads

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
